Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

-

Drop The Book is a membership based peer-to-peer (P2P) wagering platform. This means you’re betting directly against other members rather than a bookmaker, creating a fair and transparent environment.

How much does it cost?

+

Membership is only $9.99 per month plus applicable taxes.

Is Drop The Book legal?

+

Yes, our platform operates in jurisdictions where P2P betting is legally permitted. Users must comply with local laws and regulations.

Can I bet real money on Drop The Book?

+

No. Drop The Book is a social platform that allows members to track and record their bets with each other. Members are allowed to wager with each other, but the settling of all bets takes place between members privately off the platform.

What can I bet on?

+

Anything. Members can wager on sporting events, political outcomes, and personal challenges. All bets can be customized by each member.

What if a member doesn't pay?

+

Drop The Book does not hold or escrow any funds. All bets between members are settled independently.
